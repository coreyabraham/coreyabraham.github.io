Brief

"Typical Custom Engine" is a C++ Engine that I've been working on in the small free time I get every now and then between my university work and the part time job that I do outside of the university course. I mainly work on this to showcase everything I've learnt while working here, as well as adding on top of that pre-established knowledge with my own works so I can truly experiment with what I can do without needing prior resources to do it!

In it's current form, it's at it's most base level since I haven't worked on it as much as I wished, mainly in part to that free time I mentioned being quite slim, however I plan on working on it throughout 2025 as well, so be sure to look for more commits going back and forward on the GitHub linked at the bottom of the page!
